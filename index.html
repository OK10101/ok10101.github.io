About me

Hello there! 👋 My name is Oleh and I'm a Senior Full-Stack Developer and DevOps Engineer originally from Ukraine with over 9 years of experience delivering scalable, secure, and high-performance applications across a wide range of industries — especially eCommerce.

I believe in thoughtful architecture, clean code, and full ownership of my work.
